Wifi-meter

Wifi-meter is an art project that shows how many wifi-networks are in the environemnt. A Raspberry Pi is measuring the wifi-networks and controls three PCF8574AP Philips IIC port expander. The signals from the port expanders goes to three ULN2803 darlington transisor arrays that drives 24 5mm blue leds.
